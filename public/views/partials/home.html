Meet our Ferrets and Critters! They want to meet you too. Check out some of our success stories below:

Waldo and Aldo were meant for each other. Once we got wind of these two in search of warmth, we knew they could warm each others' hearts, and faces.

Angelina was rescued from a junkyard, where she'd been taking refuge in a nest made of polyester rags. She was recently adopted and now only wears cashmere.

Huxley was brought to Ferral Ferret Farm with two emaciated raccoons. Henriette adopted all three! Huxley now lives a happy life filled with raccoon raucous and ruckus.

Isadore was a service ferret in training that didn't make the cut and ended up here instead. He was adopted by a former cop. Together, they are a great duo and enjoy retirement.

Connor has issues and does not get along well with anybody, not even other ferrets. Luckily, the CEO of a jungle gym company recently adopted Connor and built him many toys since.

Spartina came to us with a crooked tail and the flu. Ferret Farm got her tail straightened out to improve her balance. She now has an inhaler, and works as a Shutterstock model with her person, a professional photographer.
